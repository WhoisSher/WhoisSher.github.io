Indonesian Chicken Soup With Noodles, Turmeric and Ginger (Soto Ayam)


If you are a big fan of noddle soups, you should definitely try this!
Soto ayam, an Indonesian version of chicken soup, is a clear herbal broth brightened by fresh turmeric and herbs, with vermicelli noodles buried in the bowl, and is hearty enough for a meal. Sometimes I like to eat it with a bowl of hot steamy rice. Soto ayam is one of my favourite Indonesian dish as well as my comfort food. There are so many types of soto ayam and a lot of different ways to make them. I will be sharing my mom's homemade soto ayam recipe. I hope you'll like them!
Soup Base:
  • 1 whole chicken divided into 4 parts
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 1 tomato
  • 12 oz rice vermicelli
  • 1 liter (4 cups) of water
  • 2 tbs of vegetable/olive/canola oil

Spice Ingredients:
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 5 shallots
  • a finger of tumeric
  • a piece of ginger
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass
  • 3 pieces of candlenuts
  • 3 pieces of bay leaves
  • 5 pieces of lime leaves

Garnish:
  • 2/3 cup of fresh bean sprouts
  • 4 tbsp fresh cilantro optional
  • 1 stalk green onion sliced
  • 2 tbsp crispy fried onions optional
How to Make the Spice:
  1. Place garlic, shallots, chilies, candlenuts, turmeric, 1 tsp salt, 3 tsp water altogether into a small food processor or a blender. Blend it until smooth.
  2. In a pan, add 2 tbs of vegetable/olive/canola oil and the blended spice together. Stir-fry for around 1-2 minutes in medium heat.
  3. Then add the chopped lemongrass, bay leaves, lime leaves and sliced ginger to the spice paste in the pan. Cook around 1-2 minutes until fragrant and brown.

How to Make Soup Base:
  1. In a pot, boil 1 liter (4 cups) of water. Then add the chicken. Boil for approximately 30 minutes until the chicken is soft and tender.
  2. While waiting for the chicken, cut the potatoes into cubes and dice the tomatoes.
  3. After the chicken is already soft, remove it from the pot. Let it cool a bit and start shredding them.
  4. Take the potatoes and put them into the boiling chicken stock, cook until soft.
  5. Put the spice paste into the chicken broth with the potatoes in the pot. Mix them and bring them into a boil for about 1-2 minutes.
  6. Lastly, soak the vermicelli noodles in boiling water for about 3-5 minutes. Make sure to not soak it too long or else it will be soggy.

How to Prepare:

  1. Prepare a bowl filled with vermicelli noodle and shredded chicken on top
  2. Then pour the soup over top with the potatoes on the bowl. You can add the ammount according to your preferrence.
  3. Finally, garnish and enjoy!


    4. Notes: Add more oil or a small splash of water as needed if the spice mixture won't blend. You can also add toppings like boiled eggs, and add lime to enhance the flavour of the soup!
